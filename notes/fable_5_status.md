# What Happened to Fable 5?

## Executive Summary
There was never officially a "Fable 5" - the next mainline entry was reimagined as a reboot simply titled "Fable" (sometimes called Fable 4 by fans). This game has been delayed from 2026 to February 2027.

## Background: The Original Fable Series
- **Fable (2004)** - Original Xbox launch title by Lionhead Studios
- **Fable II (2008)** - Xbox 360 sequel
- **Fable III (2010)** - Xbox 360 final mainline entry
- Series features Albion setting, morality choices, life simulation elements

## The Failed Fable Legends
- Fable Legends was a planned spin-off for Xbox One/Windows
- Development cancelled on March 7, 2016 when Lionhead Studios closed
- Microsoft reportedly invested $75 million, making it one of the most expensive cancelled games
- Closure marked the end of Lionhead Studios after 17 years

## The Next Mainline Entry: Reboot vs. Sequel Decision
- **April 2017**: Phil Spencer hinted at new Fable possibilities
- **2017**: Playground Games created a second team to work on Fable
- **Decision**: Playground chose to reboot rather than continue as "Fable 4" or "Fable 5"
- **Rationale**: Felt it would be "inauthentic" to continue Lionhead's work with different studio culture
- **Result**: New story in Albion with new protagonist, simply titled "Fable"

## Development Timeline
- **2020**: Officially announced at Xbox Games Showcase as "new beginning"
- **2026 target**: Originally scheduled for Autumn 2026 release
- **February 2027**: Now delayed to give game its own launch window
- **Reason**: Xbox's crowded release schedule including GTA VI, Halo, Gears of War
- **Status**: Development ongoing, game rated and nearing completion

## Key Differences from Original Series
- Modern graphics and open-world design
- Cross-platform release (PS5, Xbox Series X/S, PC)
- Game Pass availability at launch
- New protagonist and story (not continuing original hero's journey)
- Developed by Playground Games (Forza Horizon studio) instead of Lionhead

## Current Status (as of 2026)
- Game is complete and undergoing final polish
- Recent ratings suggest development nearing completion
- Will launch February 2027 alongside major Xbox showcase
- First Fable game on PlayStation platforms
- Expected to be one of Microsoft's biggest first-party releases

## Conclusion
The "Fable 5" that fans anticipated never materialized because Microsoft and Playground Games decided to reboot the franchise instead of creating a direct sequel. The upcoming 2027 title represents a fresh start for the series, learning from the original games while using modern development capabilities.
